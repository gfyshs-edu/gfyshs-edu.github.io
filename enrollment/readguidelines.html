Guidelines and Instructions for Senior High School Applicants

 

  1. Application to the General Flaviano Yengko Senior High School is done online.
  1. Only students who are currently enrolled in Grade 10 may apply.
  1. Complete the online application. Refer to the Task List in the Activity Log.
  1. Upload a 2" x 2" ID Picture.
  2. Update current school information.
  3. Encode grades.
  4. Upload all required documents.

    - 2" x 2" picture

    - PSA/NSO Birth Certificate or Passport

    - Form 137

    - Good moral

    - Barangay Certification

    - Certificate Of Recognition

    1. Certify and fully understand the application conforme then click the Submit Verification button.
    1. Feel free to Message us at https://www.facebook.com/YengkoSHS/ for any inquiry regarding the application procedure.
