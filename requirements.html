Guidelines and Instructions for Senior High School Applicants Online

  1. Application to the General Flaviano Yengko Senior High School is done online.
  2. Only students who are currently enrolled in Grade 10 may apply.
  3. Complete the online application. Refer to the Task List in the Activity Log.
    • Upload your 2" x 2" ID Picture.
    • Update your current school information.
    • Encode your grades.
    • Upload all of the required documents.
  4. Certify and fully understand the application conforme then click the Submit Verification button.
  5. If you have any questions or need clarification, please visit and message the school's Facebook page.
    6. Visit the Facebook page

Guidelines and Instructions for Senior High School Applicants to Enroll in the School (Face-to-face)

  1. Proceed to help desk/information center and secure queue number and enrollment form; Fill up the enrollment form and wait to be called.
    • Ask the client log and then provide the queue number and enrollment form.

    • Person In-Charge: Help Desk Officer/CSU

      • Form: Queue Number

  2. Go to table 1 and present all the needed documents to the frontline officer for validation of requirements. Ask for checklist and evident of acceptance of documents.
    • Accept and validate all the documents needed. Ensure documents presented are complete. Provide the client evident of acceptance.

    • Person In-Charge: Frontline Officer 1

      • Form: Filled Out Enrollment Form and Documents

  3. Proceed to table 2 for sectioning (TVL learners).

    • Encode the name of the learner to the class/section the client belongs (TVL).

    • Person In-Charge: Frontline Officer 2

      • Form: Student Masterlist

    Proceed to Computer room for Entrance Assessment Examination (STEM Learners); wait for the result and proceed to Table 2 for sectioning.

    • Encode the name of the learner to the class/section the client belongs (STEM Learners).

    • Person In-Charge: ICT Teacher and Frontline Officer 2

      • Form: Student Masterlist and Examination Result


  4. Secure temporary identification card and inquire about subject requirements class schedule, schedule of school orientation and school uniform.
    • Furnish the client the class schedule and subject requirements. Inform them also about school uniform and orientation.

    • Person In-Charge: Frontline Officer 3

      • Form: Class Schedule and list of subject requirements

Guidelines and Instructions for Transferee Applicants (Face-to-face)

  1. Proceed to help desk/information center and secure a transaction number of enrollment form (transferred in); Fill out the enrollment form and wait to be called.
    • Ask the client to log and then provide the transaction number and enrollment form.

    • Person In-Charge: Help Desk Officer/CSU

      • Form: Transaction Number
    Proceed to help desk/information center and secure a transaction number and wait to be called.
    • Ask the client to log and then provide the transaction number.

    • Person In-Charge: Help Desk Officer/CSU

      • Form: Transaction Number

  2. For Transferred -IN: Go to table 1 and present all the needed documents to the frontline officer for validation of requirements. Ask for checklist and evident of acceptance of documents.

    • Accept and validate all the needed documents. Ensure documents presented are complete. Provide the client evident of acceptance.

    • Person In-Charge: Frontline Officer 1

      • Form: Filled Out Enrollment Form and Documents

    For Transferred -OUT: Present all the needed documents to the guidance teacher for validation of requirements.

    • Accept and validate all the the needed documents. Ensure documents presented are compltete. Refer the client to the school head.

    • Person In-Charge: Guidance Teacher

      • Form: Students' Clearance
  3. Proceed to the office of the school principal for an interview.
    • Interview both the learner and parents.

    • Person In-Charge: School Principal

      • Form: Note/Remarks of School Head
  4. Proceed to table 2 for sectioning (Transferred -IN)
    • Encode the name of the learner to the class/section the client belongs.

    • Person In-Charge: Frontline Officer 2

      • Form: Students' Masterlist
