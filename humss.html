More About HUMSS

This section shares information about the HUMMS strand. Here you will also learn about the possible courses in college and careers that you can pursue in the future.

Humanities and Social Sciences

The HUMMS strand provides students with a broad range of disciplines by applying their experiences and talents in the exploration and inquiry of human circumstances through the study of behavior and social changes.

HUMSS will deepen each student's grasp of society, politics, literature, arts, and culture. Your understanding of human civilizations will be enhanced by this track, which also enables the students to recognize the similarities and differences among cultures. The practical application offer may provide you with a competitive edge in addition to these high-level insights by helping you develop your professional skill set.
